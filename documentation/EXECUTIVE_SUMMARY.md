# Campus Connect: Executive Summary
## Project Proposal for Guide/Supervisor

---

## Project Overview

**Project Name:** Campus Connect - Intelligent Grievance Management System  
**Team Size:** 3 Members  
**Duration:** 16-20 weeks (4-5 months)  
**Current Status:** Phase 1 & 2 Complete (60%), Phase 3 In Progress

### Problem Statement
Traditional grievance management systems lack intelligent routing, prioritization, and transparency, leading to delayed responses and poor citizen satisfaction.

### Solution
An AI-powered mobile application that automatically routes problems to appropriate departments, prioritizes critical issues, and provides complete transparency through status tracking.
